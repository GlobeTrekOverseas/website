Our Story

GlobeTrek Overseas was born out of a shared dream — to make global education simple, accessible, and transparent for every student.

Over the years, we have guided aspiring learners to universities across continents, helping them shape their careers and futures with confidence.

What sets us apart is not just our network of global universities, but the personal touch we bring to each student's journey.

For us, every success story is personal — because when a student achieves their dream, it becomes our achievement too.

Our Mission

At GlobeTrek Overseas, our mission is to empower students with the right opportunities and guide them at every step of their study-abroad journey.

We believe that education is not just about gaining knowledge but also about broadening one's perspective, discovering potential, and shaping a brighter future.

Our mission is to simplify this journey for every student. We provide personalized counseling, accurate information, and practical support to ensure that students make informed decisions.

×

Our Vision

Our vision is to be India's most student-centric consultancy, known for integrity, success, and long-lasting relationships.

We envision a future where every student, regardless of background, has equal access to international education.

We aim to become the trusted partner of every aspiring student who dreams of pursuing education overseas. To achieve this, we constantly innovate our processes, update our knowledge base, and maintain strong partnerships with globally recognized universities.

×

Core Values

The foundation of GlobeTrek Overseas is built on three core values:

  • Honesty We believe in being truthful and ethical in all our dealings. Students and parents place immense trust in us, and we honor that by providing genuine advice and realistic guidance.
  • Transparency We maintain complete openness in our processes, documentation, and communication. From fee structures to admission chances, every detail is shared clearly.
  • Dedication Our team works tirelessly to support students in achieving their goals. From the first counseling session to the final pre-departure briefing, we remain committed.
