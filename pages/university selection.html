University Selection & Applications

Your pathway to the perfect university experience

Find Your Ideal University

At Globetrek Overseas, we guide you in selecting the perfect university and navigating the application process, ensuring alignment with your academic and career aspirations.

University Selection Criteria

Reputation

Research rankings and reputation of universities, especially in your field of interest. Check international university rankings and student reviews.

Course Offering

Ensure the university offers the specific course or program you're interested in.

Faculty Expertise

Investigate the faculty's qualifications, research work, and industry connections.

Location & Campus Life

Consider campus facilities, location (urban or rural), and extracurricular opportunities. International student support services are crucial for a smooth transition.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Look for universities offering scholarships or financial aid for international students.

Our Services

University/College Selection

Assistance in selecting the right university or college based on your preferences, budget, and career goals.

Course Guidance

Expert advice on choosing the most suitable courses that align with career ambitions and market trends.

Application Assistance

Detailed support with university and college applications, ensuring your personal statement, academic documents, and portfolio are up to the mark.

Visa Application Assistance

Guidance on compiling necessary documents, submitting visa applications, and preparing for interviews.

Pre-Departure Briefing

Detailed instructions on what to expect before, during, and after arrival, including tips on adjusting to life in the new country.

Application Steps

1. Consultation & Initial Assessment

You share your preferences, academic background, and goals. We recommend the best options tailored to your needs.

2. Shortlisting Institutions and Courses

Based on the consultation, we curate a tailored list of institutions and programs.

3. Application Process

We prepare your documents and submit applications to the shortlisted institutions.

4. Visa Processing

Once you receive an offer letter, we initiate the visa process.

5. Pre-Departure Orientation

We provide guidance on accommodation, travel arrangements, and other essentials.

Post-Admission Services

Accommodation Assistance

Help in finding suitable housing near the educational institution.

Travel Arrangements

Guidance on booking flights and arranging for airport pickups or transportation.

Orientation Programs

Helping you adjust to life in a new country with cultural tips, student support services, and more.
